Teaching Philosophy

As an instructor for Discrete Structures - I in Computer Science department, my philosophy has always been that the most important goal for students in my course to gain a fundamental understanding of the concepts behind the material, and to know how and when these concepts may be applied, both within Computer Science and beyond it. To achieve these goals, I employ a variety of techniques to accommodate the different backgrounds, learning styles, and academic/career goals of students ...
